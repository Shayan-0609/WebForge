# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in WebForge a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are committed to creating a community that is friendly, collaborative, and professional.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language  
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences  
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism  
- Focusing on what is best for the community  
- Showing empathy towards other community members  

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form  
- Disrespectful or offensive comments or actions  
- Publishing others’ private information without permission  
- Disruptive behavior that harms collaboration or productivity  

---

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the maintainers at **shayanzia4@gmail.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.

Maintainers are expected to follow these Code of Conduct guidelines in all interactions.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies both within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community. Examples of representing a project include using an official project e-mail address, posting via an official social media account, or acting as an appointed representative at an online or offline event.
